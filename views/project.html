Project Overview

PocketDoc allows you to connect with your doctor and perform checkups on your phone in comfort of your home!

Last year in the United States alone over eighteen million adults living outside of nursing homes and other assistance giving facilities reported they had trouble with mobility. For these people it can be stressful, time consuming, or even painful to go in for checkups with their doctors. We created PocketDoc to help reduce the number of doctor visits these people need.

Schedule and view appointments online. No more phone tag.

Be the number one advocate for your health

  • Quickly record and send your health data to your doctor
  • Unlimited access to your health information on your own time
  • Graph readings and track your progress

Contact your doctor through video, voice or text.
